# ErgoAssess Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 2024

## Overview

ErgoAssess is a desktop application for ergonomic posture assessment using RULA and REBA methodologies. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**ErgoAssess does NOT collect any personal data.**

### What we DON'T do:
- ❌ We do not collect personal information
- ❌ We do not track usage analytics
- ❌ We do not send data to external servers
- ❌ We do not use cookies or tracking technologies
- ❌ We do not access your internet connection

### How the app works:
- ✅ All image processing happens locally on your device
- ✅ Uploaded images are only stored temporarily during analysis
- ✅ No data leaves your computer
- ✅ 100% offline operation

## Data Storage

- Images uploaded for analysis are processed in memory
- Temporary files may be created in your system's temp folder and are automatically deleted
- Generated PDF reports are saved only where you choose to save them

## Third-Party Services

ErgoAssess does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

## Children's Privacy

ErgoAssess is a professional tool intended for workplace safety assessments. It does not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
